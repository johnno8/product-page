Since it was founded in 1972 Giant has become the world’s largest producer of high-quality bikes. It has long been an innovator, introducing lighter, stronger aluminum frames at a time when the industry standard was steel. Giant were first to make carbon fibre bikes widely available and helped to define the look and feel of modern road racing bikes.

Today, this spirit of innovation is stronger than ever. Giant sponsor both road and mountain bike racing teams. Their collection of gear and apparel, developed and tested by some of the top teams and athletes in pro racing, continues to expand. Giant products are available from more than 12,000 retail stores worldwide, or from their website at www.giant-bicycles.com
